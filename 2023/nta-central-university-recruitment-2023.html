How to apply?

  • Apply for NTA CUREC 2023 Exam: Online from Dec 01-21.
  • Read the job notice before applying for NTA CUREC positions.
  • Get your documents ready: check eligibility, ID, and address details.
  • Scan your documents (photo, sign, ID) for the application.
  • Review your form before submitting to make sure everything is correct.
  • Pay the application fee for your form to be complete.
  • Only complete forms with paid fees will be accepted.
  • Print a copy of your final submitted form for your records.
